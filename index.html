The Small Group...

...is a group setup for passionate Christian who are tired of all the status quo and have burning desires to know God and experience His presence more daily through bible-led discussions, prayers and fellowships.

God is not 'dead,' 'absent' or 'missing' from the world today...our attention and care have shifted and as result He feels distant. If you want the fire back in, then join us and make this community one we 'reason together' on basis of God's word.

Our Belief

We believe in God...

...we believe in Jesus as His son and believe that Jesus died, resurrected and now lives for us before The Father. We believe there is salvation through no other but Jesus and that The Father only welcomes us if our faith is founded on Jesus.

"Because, if you confess with your mouth that Jesus is Lord and believe in your heart that God raised him from the dead, you will be saved.
For with the heart one believes and is justified, and with the mouth one confesses and is saved." - Romans 10:9-10
